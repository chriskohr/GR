Annual Fun Fest Carnival and Bootique

Don't Miss This Halloween Family Fun! Join us on October 26th for a day filled with games, food and prizes!

Here are just a few of the awesome events planned for Funfest:

  • Cakewalk with delicious prizes
  • Fun games for all ages 
  • Delicious lunch and sweets 
  • AMAZING homemade Halloween and everyday crafts (and so much more) can be found at our
    “shop til you drop” Bootique.
  • And don’t worry about cooking dinner that evening because you can always purchase a delicious homemade dinner and dessert while browsing the Bootique!

You can help in so many ways, there's a job for everyone! Donate cakes for the cakewalk, crafts, desserts/dinners for the Bootique, and make sure to sign up to help with your class booth.

Stop by the Funfest booth at Kick-Off Day and sign up to volunteer! Class booth sign-up sheets will be available at Back to School night and we ask that every family participate.
