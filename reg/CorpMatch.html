Corporate Match

Making your Glorietta donations go twice the distance is easy - many companies match their employees' charitable contributions, and some companies even match the gifts of spouses or retirees. You can double, and sometimes even triple, your charitable impact simply by filling out and sending our Glorietta Corporate Match team a matching gift form from your employer. Our team will handle it from there.

See the employment benefits page on your company's intranet or contact your human resources department to obtain a matching gift form and get information on your employer's corporate match policy.

And you can click here for a list of the Bay Area companies that currently offer charitable gift matching.
