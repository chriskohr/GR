¡Hola! ¿Cómo estás?

Glorietta’s popular fee-based Spanish Language Program is back for its eighth year! This year the program will include the following classes: Introduction to Spanish (for Kindergarteners 4x per week), Beginning Spanish (for First Graders 4x per week), Intermediate Spanish (for 2nd & 3rd graders 4x per week) and Advanced Spanish (for 4th & 5th graders 2x per week).

The Glorietta program is academically based and presented in an environment which is engaging, stimulating and fun. Through our Passport Program, Spanish culture is also incorporated into the curriculum. Assessments and regular progress reports are provided.

According to studies, children up to the age of puberty learn languages much more easily.
If you are interested in exposing your child to the benefits of learning a foreign language, watch for our registration announcements in mid-September.
