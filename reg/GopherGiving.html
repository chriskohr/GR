FUNDRAISING
Gopher Giving   

Gopher Giving , Glorietta's 1st Annual Giving Campaign, is being introduced this fall to provide families with more options in how they contribute to the school.

Our Annual Dinner Auction, with this year's theme of A Night at the Oscars, is our primary (and very fun!) fundraiser for Glorietta. Some families, however, are unable to attend this event, or prefer to make a monetary donation rather than bid at the auction, or would like to make a monetary contribution in the fall (2012) and still participate in the auction in the spring (2013). With the introduction of Gopher Giving, we are now able to accommodate all these preferences.

The purpose of Gopher Giving is to raise immediate income to help bridge the gap between what the state provides and what it really costs to deliver an outstanding education. Our suggested donation amount is $700 per student. Every contribution, regardless of size, is greatly appreciated…and needed! Our goal is to achieve 100% participation from Glorietta families in our fundraising opportunities - Gopher Giving and/or the Annual Dinner Auction.

The names of all Gopher Giving donors will be listed in various Glorietta Parents' Club publications unless we receive a donor's request to remain anonymous. We will not publish donation amounts, only donors' names. All donors will be issued a letter of appreciation that will serve as a receipt. Since the GPC (Glorietta Parents' Club) is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization, Gopher Giving donations are fully tax deductible as provided by law and are eligible for Corporate Match.

Friends and family members are welcome to participate in Gopher Giving. They can use your child's student ID number to make a credit card donation through our online Gopher Store, accessible via the Glorietta website OR they can mail a check donation, payable to Glorietta Parents' Club, to:
Gopher Giving
15 Martha Road
Orinda, CA 94563

Our Glorietta students and teachers thank you for your support, and the Glorietta Parents' Club welcomes you to our community of giving!
