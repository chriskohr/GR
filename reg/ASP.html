Glorietta 2012-2013 After School Enrichment Program (ASE)

The After School Enrichment Program (ASE) at Glorietta is a terrific way to enrich your child’s Glorietta experience!  We offer a wide and varied selection of fee-based activities that support your kids’ favorite hobbies and expose them to new ones.  It’s a great way for them to connect with old friends and make new ones with common interests.

New in 2012-2013, most classes will be offered from 3:30 pm. to 4:30 pm. On Early Release Mondays, classes will begin at 2:30 pm. Each session will also offer one Kinder-Only class at 2:00 pm, which will rotate by session. We encourage you send your students to Gopher Club while awaiting the start of their ASE Classes.

Beginning in October, courses are offered in Fall, Winter, and Spring sessions and fall under the categories of Fine Arts, Performing Arts, Home Economics, Sports, and Science and Games. Vendors include award-winning providers like Berkeley Chess School, The Art Room, Mad Science, and Funkmode-and all are conveniently coming to Glorietta so you don't have to drive to them to class! Courses vary from session to session, but for a sample, visit last year's Spring Catalog by clicking here . The current session catalog will be made available to parents as soon as it has been finalized and approved.

Registration for each of the three sessions is conducted separately through the on-line Glorietta Gopher Store. Check the Gabette and Glorietta e-mails for deadlines, reminders, and updates.
