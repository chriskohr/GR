Glorietta 2013-2014 After School Enrichment Program (ASE)

Many Glorietta students have truly enjoyed their After School Enrichment (ASE) experiences. This program is in place for no other reason than to benefit OUR CHILDREN, "enriching" their Glorietta experience. We hope you enjoy this convenient service, which is not designed to raise funds. Prices are passed on directly from the vendors, with nominal administrative fees. Unless noted, the courses are also not designed by Glorietta staff or volunteers. Our role is to facilitate the program, pairing dynamic and challenging outside vendors with appropriately interested students!

Please review the ASE Program Policies by clicking here. The link will provide detailed information regarding registration, drop off and pickup, behavioral issues, and class cancelations. For further assistance, contact Meredith Friedman or Donna Pickthall.

The approximate dates of the three sessions are:
Fall: Registration 9/23-10/4; Classes 10/15-12/18
Winter: Registration 12/9-1/10; Classes 1/13-3/14
Spring: Registration 3/17-4/11; Classes 4/14-5/30
