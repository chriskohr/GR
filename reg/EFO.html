Educational Foundation of Orinda(EFO)

The Educational Foundation of Orinda's commitment to Glorietta students:

  • Art Instruction (100% EFO funded)
  • Music Instruction (100% EFO funded)
  • Instrumental Music Instruction (100% EFO funded)
  • K-3 class size reduction (Joint funding from Glorietta Parents' Club and EFO)

By participating in the EFO Campaign at Glorietta you not only ensure essential academic and enrichment programs are sustained, but help provide a margin of excellence not found in other school districts.

Please donate today and remember you can double your gift with corporate matching! Every donation is truly valued, appreciated and needed.
