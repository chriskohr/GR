Traffic Safety & Carpool Procedures

Our goal is to provide our children with the safest possible traffic environment at and around Glorietta School. During the first few weeks of school when most students are “Early Birds” (except Kindergarteners), carpool traffic is very heavy. It is especially important to allow sufficient time to get to school and to obey all the safety rules. (This also applies throughout the year on Mondays when all students are on the “Early Bird” schedule.) To ensure the efficiency of Glorietta’s carpool system, please follow the procedures outlined below.

CARPOOLING:

  1. Drop off/pick up children ONLY in the designated carpool area, which is the main school parking lot in front of the school office. Do not allow your child/ren to exit your vehicle along Martha Road or Hillcrest.
  2. When approaching the school for carpool drop off/pick up, always proceed to the turnaround near the UPPER parking lot and then enter the main school parking lot after coming back down Martha Road.
  3. PLEASE DISCONTINUE CELL PHONE USE PRIOR TO ENTERING THE CARPOOL AREA.
  4. After entering the carpool area, always proceed to the furthest available spot in the carpool line. This allows as many cars as possible to enter the drop off/pick up area at one time. Please pay attention and follow the directions of the staff member on carpool duty.
  5. Please DO NOT LEAVE YOUR VEHICLE UNATTENDED in the carpool area. It is hazardous to others if a parked car is blocking the traffic flow. The staff member will locate and escort your child/ren to your vehicle.
  6. Please STAY IN YOUR VEHICLE when dropping off/picking up your child/ren. The staff member will assist your child/ren in getting into the vehicle if necessary.
  7. All students have a designated area to sit and wait for their rides. First, second, and third grade students are in the hallway on your right as you enter the driveway in front of the school. The fourth and fifth grade students are seated on the benches directly in front of the main office. All the children are seated and facing the driveway looking for their rides.
  8. When exiting the parking lot, always turn RIGHT onto Martha Road.
  9. Remember that children need to exit on the right side of the vehicle, so please take this into consideration when making seating arrangements.
  10. Always make sure everyone in your vehicle is wearing a seat belt.
  11. Please review these procedures with anyone who will be handling carpool at Glorietta for you.

PARK AND WALK:

If you choose to park your car on the street or in the upper parking lot (space permitting), please escort your child/ren to the school grounds.

PLEASE DO NOT:

  • Use the upper parking lot for drop off/pick up.
  • Park in handicapped parking area without an appropriate permit.
  • Bypass the turn-around, and thereby “cut” in front your fellow parents.

PLEASE BE ALERT!
