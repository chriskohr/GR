ROOM PARENT

Are you looking for a fun way to get involved in your child's class while getting to know the teacher and other parents? Then becoming a Room Parent is THE job for you! You would work directly with your child's teacher coordinating volunteers for class activities like holiday parties, field trip chaperones, auction, Fun Fest, and any exciting special projects your class will have. You will find many rewards in taking on this position. Look for sign-up sheets at the Room Parent table at Kick-Off Day.

OUTDOOR EXPLORERS/NATURE PARENT

Do you enjoy fresh air and helping your child think like a scientist and learn about the world in our outdoor classroom? Consider volunteering to be the Outdoor Explorer parent this year. Each class has two volunteers that work together to take the students on walks around our very own outdoor classroom around Glorietta’s campus. Help our kids discover science in the woods in our very own seated amphitheater by the creek and science in our unbelievable sensory garden. This is a once a month commitment and the program is easy to follow. Plus, it’s a treat to follow the seasons through the eyes of our children.

Look for more information at Back to School Night or contact:
   Tammi McCormick at tammilmc@comcast.net

THINK FIRST

Do you want to be a familiar face in the classroom, get to know your child’s peers and have fun in the process? How about becoming a Think First volunteer? Think First is a program that teaches children empathy skills, impulse control and anger management for all grade levels. This is a highly rewarding once-a-week teaching commitment for approximately the first half of the school year, starting in October. The smiles, greetings and hugs from your "students" are worth their weight in gopher gold! Training and all of the lesson plans are provided.
