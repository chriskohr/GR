GLORIETTA ONLINE SCHOOL REGISTRATION INVOLVES 3 STEPS: READ, PAY AND PRINT

1. READ

STEP 1: Follow the "Next Page" arrows to review Glorietta School programs. Volunteers from these programs will be available at Kick-Off Day for sign-ups and questions. For your convenience, each topic is its own printable page.

 

STEP 2: On the final page, you will be provided a link to the Glorietta Gopher Store.

 

Please note: Student IDs are required for all transactions. Please make sure your Student ID(s) is handy. This information was included in the letter mailed to your home address in August. Please save this number since it will be needed to register for school programs and services throughout the school year. If this number is lost, please contact Cheryl McNair in the Glorietta School office. School office hours are 9-3, M-F.

 

 

2. PAY

CLICK ON "REGISTRATION ITEMS" in the column on the left to pay for GPC-related expenses: Glorietta Parents' Club Family Membership, Gopher Giving Donation, Gopher Club Administrative Donation, Technology in the Classroom Fund, Emergency Supply Fund, Yearbooks, Birthday Books and Additional Phone Directories.

Note: For your convenience, families with two or more children may purchase REGISTRATION ITEMS with one student ID number per family and use this ID number to make purchases for multiple children.

 

CLICK ON "GOPHER GOURMET" to make lunch plan purchases.

Note: Families with two or more children must purchase GOPHER GOURMET LUNCH by using each child's individual student ID number to specify each child's choices.

 

CLICK ON "EFO" if you wish to make a donation at this time.

 

 

3. PRINT

Once payment is finalized, a receipt will appear. Please PRINT THIS RECEIPT, then CLICK THE LINK to print seven mandatory registration forms. Bring the receipt and the completed registration forms to Kick-OFF Day on Tuesday, August 27th to finish the registration process and receive your teacher assignment.
