HELPING HANDS

When a Glorietta family needs help, GPC always pulls together to support them!

Helping Hands provides home-delivered meals to Glorietta families that may be undergoing stressful events. In addition to the volunteers who will generously deliver meals, Helping Hands provides home-delivered meals to Glorietta families that may be undergoing stressful events. In addition to the volunteers who will generously deliver meals, Helping Hands would like to have one liaison per classroom. As a liaison, you will be the Helping Hands class contact. If there is a family in need in your class, you would work with the Helping Hands Coordinator to set up volunteers and work with the family and be their contact person.

Helping Hands is looking for many generous volunteers who are willing to help out when a Glorietta family is in need.

WELCOMING COMMITTEE

We are thrilled to have many new families joining our Glorietta community each year. These children will no doubt be a bit nervous on their first day at a new school, so your role is to make it a little easier on the entire family. New families have questions about carpool, the lunch program, the school bus, etc, and we will have the answers for you! Our goal is to connect new families with "veteran" parents to help answer your questions throughout the year.

If you are a “Veteran Parent” and would like to be a member of the Welcoming Committee and help mentor a new family, please contact:
Kathryn Ishizu at kishizu@yahoo.com. You may also sign up on Kick-Off Day.

HOSPITALITY COMMITTEE

The Hospitality Committee provides culinary support to the Glorietta Parents Club by hosting a much appreciated and anticipated teacher and staff luncheon every month during the school year. This fun group also provides refreshments for the Parents Club meetings and various events throughout the year.

Each month we will have a grade provide the food for that month. This way everyone gets an opportunity to share their cooking talent with the staff. Please stop by the Hospitality table on Kick-Off Day.

WANTED - PLAYGROUND PARENTS!

Do you like to see your child in action on the playground? Would you like a chance to get to know more of our Gophers outside of the classroom setting? Sign up to be a lunch recess volunteer and help us keep the playground a fun and safe place for our children!

We need volunteer parents to work at all grade levels (1st -5th), but have the greatest need at the 1st-3rd grade levels.

As a Playground Parent, we ask that you attend a mandatory one-hour training on September 4th with Ron Langer. At this training, we will review basic playground rules, provide some tools for how to handle playground situations and share some fun and exciting new games for the playground! Whether you have volunteered in this capacity before or will be a new face on the playground, you will want to attend. We look forward to working together with you to provide a safe and fun playground for our children!

Please look for the PLAYGROUND PARENT table on Kick-Off Day to sign-up, or contact:
Jenny Hee at reh418@yahoo.com
